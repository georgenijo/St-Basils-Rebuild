St.Basil's Youth Ministry

YOUTH MINISTRY

The Youth Ministry at St. Basil's Syrian Orthodox Church is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth and development of our young people. We provide a supportive environment where youth can deepen their faith, build lasting friendships, and develop leadership skills while staying connected to their Orthodox heritage.

Our youth programs are designed for teenagers and young adults, offering opportunities for worship, fellowship, service, and spiritual formation. Through various activities and events, we aim to help our youth navigate the challenges of modern life while remaining grounded in their faith.

OUR MISSION

The mission of our Youth Ministry is to:

  • Foster a strong Orthodox Christian identity among our youth
  • Provide opportunities for spiritual growth through prayer, worship, and study
  • Build a supportive community of young believers
  • Encourage service to the church and the wider community
  • Develop future leaders for our church
  • Create meaningful connections between faith and daily life
"Let no one despise your youth, but be an example to the believers in word, in conduct, in love, in spirit, in faith, in purity." - 1 Timothy 4:12

ACTIVITIES & PROGRAMS

Our Youth Ministry offers various activities throughout the year:

  • Weekly youth meetings and Bible study sessions
  • Annual youth retreats and conferences
  • Community service projects and charitable activities
  • Cultural programs and talent shows
  • Sports activities and recreational events
  • Participation in diocesan and regional youth events
  • Leadership development workshops
  • Prayer meetings and spiritual discussions

GET INVOLVED

We welcome all young people of our parish to join our Youth Ministry. Whether you're in middle school, high school, college, or a young professional, there's a place for you in our community. Come and experience the joy of growing in faith alongside your peers!

For more information about our Youth Ministry programs and upcoming events, please contact the church office or speak with our Youth Ministry coordinators after Sunday service.
