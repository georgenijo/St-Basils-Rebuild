In Memoriam

In Loving Memory

Very Rev. Fr. Punnoose Abraham Kallamparambil

We remember with deep gratitude the late Very Rev. Fr. Punnoose Abraham Kallamparambil, whose faithful ministry was the spiritual foundation upon which St. Basil's was built. From our church's very first service until the Lord called him home, Achen served our community with unwavering devotion and pastoral love.

For seven years, he journeyed to be with us week after week, celebrating the Divine Liturgy and nourishing our souls with the sacred mysteries. Even beyond those formative years, Achen remained just one call away—always ready to serve our community whenever we needed him, embodying the true heart of a shepherd who never abandoned his flock.

His steadfast presence during our most formative years shaped not only our worship life but our very identity as a church family. We honor his memory and give thanks for the legacy of faith, dedication, and love he left with our community.

May his soul rest in peace in the embrace of our Lord.
