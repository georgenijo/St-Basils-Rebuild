St. Basil's Syriac Orthodox Church, Newton

was established in 2009. Since our founding, we have flourished as a vibrant community united by deep fellowship and unwavering faith. Our church stands as a living testament to God's presence—a sacred space where worship, prayer, and spiritual growth thrive.

We welcome people of all ages through our diverse ministries, including Sunday School for children, Youth League for young adults, Men's Prayer Fellowship, and Women's League. Each group plays a vital role in preserving our rich Orthodox traditions while fostering meaningful connections within our church family.

Whether you're seeking spiritual guidance, community fellowship, or a deeper connection to ancient Christian traditions, St. Basil's offers a warm home where faith comes alive.
