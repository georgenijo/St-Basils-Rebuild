OUR LOCATION

St. Basil's Syriac Orthodox Church is easily accessible by both car and public transportation. We're located just minutes from I-95/Route 128 and the Massachusetts Turnpike (I-90), making us convenient for visitors traveling from Boston, the North Shore, South Shore, and western suburbs. Our church is also accessible via the MBTA Green Line (D Branch). The nearest station is Eliot station, which is within walking distance or a short ride from our location.

Free parking is available in our church parking lot, accessible from Ellis Street. The lot accommodates approximately 10 vehicles. Additional street parking is available near by, as well as lot parking down the road. Please refer to the map below that specifies all available parking locations. Please be mindful of posted parking restrictions and residential permit requirements in the neighborhood. On Sundays, parking enforcement is generally relaxed, but always check street signs for specific regulations.

Take the MBTA Green Line (D Branch) to Eliot Station. From the station, it is about a 10-15 minute walk to the church. As weather is unpredictable and it gets tough to walk those 10 minutes in the colder months, we have volunteers who pick up folks from the station before Morning Prayer starts at 8:30am. If you need a ride, please don't hesitate to text or call the following number: 508.542.3003.
