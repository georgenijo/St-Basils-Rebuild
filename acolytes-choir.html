ACOLYTES & CHOIR

Our dedicated acolytes serve as essential assistants during the Divine Liturgy, helping to prepare the altar, light candles, and support our clergy in the sacred mysteries that unite us in worship. These faithful servants, ranging from children to adults, learn the ancient traditions of our Orthodox faith while contributing to the reverent atmosphere that makes our liturgical celebrations so meaningful. Our choir leads our congregation in the beautiful chants and hymns that have echoed through Syriac Orthodox churches for centuries, preserving our musical heritage while helping every voice join in praise to the Almighty. Together, these ministries embody the spirit of service and devotion that strengthens our church family, ensuring that our worship reflects both the majesty of our ancient traditions and the heartfelt participation of our community.
